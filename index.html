About Me

Hello, I’m Goatzy Games, an indie developer passionate about creating unique games in Unreal Engine 5 using C++. I also work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for web projects, though my main focus is on game development.

I have over 3 years of experience with Unreal Engine and am dedicated to designing gameplay systems, coding mechanics, and developing immersive experiences. While I handle most aspects of development myself, I occasionally collaborate with 3D artists or animators to bring my projects to life.

You can explore my completed and ongoing projects in the Projects section. If you’re interested in collaborating or discussing ideas, feel free to get in touch.

Skills

  • Game Development: Unreal Engine 5 (4+ years), C++ (2+ years)
  • Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Focus Areas: Gameplay programming, system design, level design
