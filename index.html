About Me

Hello! I’m Goatzy Games, an indie game developer. I enjoy creating and designing a wide range of 3D games using C++ and Unreal Engine—whether that’s horror games, minigames, small-scale RPGs, or pretty much anything I can imagine.

I’m always looking to deepen my knowledge of C++ and explore more of what Unreal Engine has to offer, while expanding my portfolio with projects I develop solo or in collaboration. There’s a major project I’m currently working on with a partner, which we’ll be unveiling once a demo is ready!

I genuinely enjoy designing systems and entire games with C++, not just because it’s the primary language in Unreal Engine, but because writing it is fun. Every day brings something new to learn—whether it’s C++ techniques or broader game development skills—and that’s what keeps me passionate about what I do.
