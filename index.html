About Me

Hello, I'm Goatzy Games, and I am an indie game developer making a variety of games in Unreal Engine 5 with C++ and also websites with HTML, CSS and Javascript, but I am not focused on that, currently.
I have a strong passion for creating stuff, so because of that I love creating different games and websites.

I got 3+ years of experience by now with Unreal Engine 5, but I wouldn't call myself a big professional, I do this as just a hobby, but also sometimes when I want to create something big, like I'm doing right now, I'll make sure the game is ready for even steam release. Although I'm working on everything by myself, like the coding, level design etc, I sometimes even need to hire someone to do some 3D modeling/animating or just anything art related since I once tried learning blender but the outcome was terrible so I just decided to purely focus on level design and gameplay programming.

You can look at my done projects in projects and if you would like to discuss collabing or anything else, you can contact me here.
