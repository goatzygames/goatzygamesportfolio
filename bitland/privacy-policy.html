Privacy Policy — Bitland

Last updated: November 1, 2025

This privacy policy describes how Goatzy Games collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the mobile game Bitland on Google Play.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect only the data necessary to operate Bitland and provide related services. The types of data collected include:

  • Advertising identifiers and device info: For serving and measuring ads (e.g., advertising ID, device model, OS version).
  • Crash reports and diagnostics: Automatic reports that help identify and fix bugs and crashes. These reports may include device information and logs, but not your name or other personal identifiers.
  • Purchase & transaction data: Records necessary to process in-app purchases via Google Play (purchase receipts, SKU, timestamp).

We do not require account creation, and Bitland does not itself collect names, emails, or other personally identifying information unless you contact us directly via email.

2. How We Use Information

  • To deliver and improve the game: Diagnose technical issues and optimize gameplay performance.
  • To serve ads: Provide relevant or non-personalized advertising via third-party ad networks.
  • To process purchases: Handle in-app purchases, verify transactions, and provide receipts.

3. Third-Party Services

Bitland uses third-party services that may collect or process data as described above. These services include ad networks and analytics/crash-reporting tools. We do not control their data collection and recommend reviewing their privacy policies directly. Examples of types of services commonly used:

  • Ad networks (for example: Google AdMob)
  • Crash & analytics tools (for example: Firebase Crashlytics)

Google's privacy resources: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Ads and Your Choices

Bitland contains voluntary ads. We do not force ads on players; watching ads is optional and used to support the game. Ads may be personalized based on advertising identifiers. If you prefer non-personalized ads, you can opt out or change ad personalization settings at the device level (Android > Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

5. In-App Purchases

Bitland offers optional microtransactions and in-app purchases through Google Play's billing system. We only collect purchase receipts and necessary transaction metadata to verify and fulfill purchases. Refunds and billing inquiries should be handled through Google Play's support channels.

6. Children

Bitland is designed for ages 7+ and is not directed specifically to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

7. Data Retention

We retain diagnostic and advertising-related data only as long as necessary to provide the service, for troubleshooting, measurement, and compliance purposes. Transaction records related to purchases are retained as required for purchase verification and fraud prevention.

8. Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information collected by Bitland. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure — if you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Accounts and Cloud Saves

Bitland does not require or offer user account creation or cloud saves. All game progress is stored locally on the player's device unless the player uses platform-level backup features (e.g., Android Auto Backup) outside of our control.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we'll update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of Bitland after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the data practices of Bitland, please contact:
